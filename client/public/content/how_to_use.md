# How to use this book

Pick your path — survivor or systems supporter. You do not have to read in order.

## If you are a survivor or peer

Start with **Part I**, especially Article 1 (*The Rescue Boat*). Read for recognition, not performance. Highlight what names your wiring. Skip what does not fit. Return to Article 10 when setbacks make you think you are back at zero.

## If you support or work in systems

Read Article 5 (*Translation Between the System and Trauma*), then jump to **Part IV** resources. Use the freeze protocol and 26 Laws as translation tools — not excuses, not diagnoses. Part II appendix is there when you need citations.

## What each part contains

- **Part I** — Ten counter-narratives (core value)
- **Part II** — Technical appendix (plain-language science)
- **Part III** — Missing pieces (what systems leave out)
- **Part IV** — Practical resources (protocols and heuristics)
