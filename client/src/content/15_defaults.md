# THE 15 CORE DEFAULTS
*Rules of the System*

These are the fundamental principles extracted from the manuscript. They serve as a reality check when your nervous system is offline.

1.  **Logic fails in freeze. Regulate first.**
2.  **Shame is not proof. It’s glue.**
3.  **Normal is the most addictive state for a traumatized nervous system.**
4.  **You can’t punish a survival response. You only lock it harder.**
5.  **Co-regulation is biology, not romance.**
6.  **The mirror lies when shame’s in the room.**
7.  **Addiction is a short-term regulator with long-term collateral.**
8.  **Stoicism that saves you will eventually suffocate you.**
9.  **Your nervous system doesn’t care about zip codes. It cares about predictability.**
10. **Accountability without understanding is retraumatization.**
11. **Tools must precede abstinence. Otherwise you’re just delaying the crash.**
12. **The baseline dictates the behavior. Calm feels like a setup when panic is normal.**
13. **Naming the protocol breaks its grip.**
14. **Healing is a spiral. Setbacks are data, not resets.**
15. **You don’t heal by fixing the past. You heal by upgrading the present wiring.**
