# Harm Reduction Note
*(For readers working with severe addiction)*

This book focuses on nervous‑system regulation, not specific substance‑use goals. Abstinence is one valid pathway; harm reduction is another. For readers seeking evidence‑based models, consult SAMHSA’s harm‑reduction guidelines or NAATP frameworks. Recovery isn’t one‑size‑fits‑all. The goal is regulation, not perfection.
