# Co‑Regulation Barriers & Low‑Bar Alternatives

Co‑regulation is a biological necessity, not a luxury. But steady people aren’t always available – especially in rural areas, isolated communities, or during early recovery when trust is fractured.

**If you can’t find a steady person, start with:**
- A pet (animal heart rates co‑regulate with humans)
- A recorded voice (same predictable message each time)
- A weekly phone call with a peer support line
- A digital check‑in app that asks one question: “You still breathing?”

The medium matters less than predictability. Any reliable signal that says *“You are not alone”* can become a scaffold. Use it until you can build more.
