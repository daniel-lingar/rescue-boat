The Rescue Boat & Other Counter-Narratives

A public article and manuscript hub translating trauma, addiction, shame, freeze responses, avoidance, nervous-system adaptation, and survival behavior into plain language.

This project is part of the larger What Really Happened (WRH) system from Capitol Contracts LLC. It gives readers language for patterns that are often mislabeled as weakness, laziness, defiance, noncompliance, or character failure.
